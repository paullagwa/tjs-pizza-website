For Home Cooks

Restaurant quality.
Your kitchen.

The same dough balls trusted by commercial kitchens across Australia — now available for your Friday night pizza. No experience needed.

01

Take it from the freezer

Pull a dough ball from the freezer the morning of, or defrost it quickly on the bench for 2–3 hours before you're ready to cook. No planning stress.

02

Stretch it out

Press from the centre outward with your fingertips. Lift and let gravity stretch it gently. Aim for even thickness — don't roll it, let it do the work.

03

Top it your way

Sauce, cheese, whatever's in the fridge. Less is more — 3 or 4 toppings let the base shine. Our par-baked bases are ready to top straight from the fridge.

04

Crank the oven

As hot as your oven goes — ideally 250–280°C on a baking stone or heavy tray. 8–12 minutes. Wood fire ovens are perfect: 60–90 seconds at 400°C+.

🌡
Home oven 250–280°C, baking stone or heavy tray, bottom shelf. 8–12 minutes.
🔥
Wood fire oven 400°C+. 60–90 seconds. Rotate once halfway. Our dough balls were made for this.
