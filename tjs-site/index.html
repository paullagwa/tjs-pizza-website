For Home Cooks

Restaurant quality.
Your kitchen.

The same dough balls trusted by commercial kitchens — for your Friday night pizza.

01

Take it from the freezer

Pull a dough ball out the morning of, or defrost on the bench for 2–3 hours before cooking.

02

Stretch it out

Press from the centre outward with your fingertips. Lift and let gravity do the stretching.

03

Top it your way

Sauce, cheese, whatever's in the fridge. Less is more — 3 or 4 toppings let the base shine.

04

Crank the oven

250–280°C on a baking stone, 8–12 minutes. Wood fire: 60–90 secs at 400°C+.

How long do TJ's pizza bases last?+
Our par-baked bases keep for up to 3 weeks chilled, or up to 12 months frozen. They defrost in minutes — ideal for busy service.
Are your dough balls vegan and vegetarian friendly?+
Yes — fully vegan and vegetarian friendly. They work in all types of ovens including wood fire.

No. Our products do not contain soy or milk products, making them suitable for a wide range of dietary requirements.
Can you make custom sizes and weights?+
Yes. We can make tailor-made products to custom weights and sizes, including up to half-metre squares, and special recipes under confidential contract.
Do you deliver nationally across Australia?+
Yes — based on the NSW Central Coast, we supply commercial kitchens nationally through PFD Food Services and direct.

Never. Zero added preservatives, artificial colourings, or flavours. Clean ingredients, great results.

Our dough balls work in all commercial ovens — especially wood fire. They thaw quickly, stretch easily, and hold up at high heat.
